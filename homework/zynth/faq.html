F.A.Q

What exactly can you do, zynth?

  • Graphics and motion graphics for content creators on any platform.
  • Illustration for various projects including tattoos, posters, books, stickers etc.
  • Brand campaigns and Company Rebranding.

Where can I find information about how much something will cost?

THIS PAGE includes pricing options for common projects. If something is not listed on this page feel free to E-mail me asking about it. Make sure that you are including as much detail in your E-mail as possible and I will get back to you as quickly as I can.

How does your pricing work?

All quoted prices are final and non-negotiable. Projects will be started when at least half of the agreed upon price is delivered. Paying full price up front will not raise your current place in line.

How can I get in touch with ZYNTH to further discuss plans and pricing?

All business inquiries and general questions and comments about projects, pricing and progress can be sent to ZYNTHDESIGN@GMAIL.COM

How quickly will the work that I ask for be completed?

All client work is completed in the order it was received. If there is a long list of clients ahead of you it could take longer to complete, but it will not be forgotten or ignored.
