OUR MISSION

We here at Pour Bastard’s Brewing Co. constantly strive to produce a positive impact in our community. Half of our proceeds go to assisting wildlife conservation agencies because we believe that animals should not be caged, harmed, or abused in any way, shape, or form. This paragraph is basically going to say something about how great we are and make you want to click up on the careers tab so you can join our team. Honestly though you don’t have to, we’re probably not the right fit for your career path. I mean do you really want to be a bartender? Do you want to have to deal with grown adults getting drunk off our beer and acting like children? This sentance only exists so we don’t end our mission statement with a question. Go Ducks.
